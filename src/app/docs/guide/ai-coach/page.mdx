# The AI Coach

<Note title="In development">
The coach is an **early preview**, not a finished feature. In the hosted demo you'll see
seeded examples of what a coach review looks like; live, on-your-trades coaching is still
being built. This page describes where it's headed so the direction is clear.
</Note>

The coach is a post-trade review assistant that reads the context you've already
captured — your trades, notes, tags, charts, and rules — and helps summarize what worked,
what didn't, and what to focus on next. The aim is feedback that's specific to *you* rather
than generic trading advice.

## Codify your edge first

The coach works best against a standard *you* define. The planned home for that is a setup
workspace in **Settings**, where you'll be able to:

- add your own language-model API key (the coach runs on your key, on your machine),
- complete a short trader self-assessment,
- define your rules and setups, and
- tune how the coach reviews.

That rubric is what turns "was this a good trade?" into "did this match *my* A+ setup?"

## How a review will work

Once your rules are in place, asking the coach to review a trade or a day will:

- read the trade against your criteria,
- point to where you followed your plan and where you drifted, and
- draft a recap and feedback in your voice.

## You always stay in control

This principle holds throughout the design:

- The coach **proposes**; it never posts on its own.
- Every recap and note arrives as a **draft** you read and edit.
- It runs locally against your own API key — your trades aren't handed to a hosted service
  to make this work.

## What you can do today

While the coach matures, the most valuable thing is to build the habit it depends on: import
your trades, [write daily recaps, and tag consistently](/docs/guide/recaps-and-tags). That
context is exactly what makes the coach useful when it lands — follow the
[Roadmap](/docs/roadmap) to see it come together.
