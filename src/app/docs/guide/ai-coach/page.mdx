# The AI Coach

<Note title="In development">
The coach is an **early version**, not a finished feature. An early form of live review
already works: configure your own OpenAI API key locally and the coach can draft a review
of your actual trades today. The hosted demo shows seeded examples of what that looks
like. The guided setup, other model providers, and deeper rule-checking are still being
built — this page describes both what works now and where it's headed.
</Note>

The coach is a post-trade review assistant that reads the context you've already
captured — your trades, notes, tags, charts, and rules — and helps summarize what worked,
what didn't, and what to focus on next. The aim is feedback that's specific to *you* rather
than generic trading advice.

## Codify your edge first

The coach works best against a standard *you* define. The home for that is a setup
workspace in **Settings** — the first pieces (connecting a model provider and editing
your playbook) are there today, and the rest is filling in:

- add your own language-model API key (OpenAI works today; other providers are planned),
- complete a short trader self-assessment *(coming)*,
- define your rules and setups, and
- tune how the coach reviews *(coming)*.

That rubric is what turns "was this a good trade?" into "did this match *my* A+ setup?"

## How a review works

With a model key configured, asking the coach to review a day will:

- read your trades against your playbook and criteria,
- point to where you followed your plan and where you drifted, and
- draft a recap and feedback in your voice.

## You always stay in control

This principle holds throughout the design:

- The coach **proposes**; it never posts on its own.
- Every recap and note arrives as a **draft** you read and edit.
- Nothing is sent anywhere unless *you* ask for a review. When you do, the trades, notes,
  and tags for that session go to the model provider **you** configured, under **your own
  API key** — never through our servers, and never automatically in the background. No
  key configured means nothing ever leaves your machine.

## What you can do today

While the coach matures, the most valuable thing is to build the habit it depends on: import
your trades, [write daily recaps, and tag consistently](/docs/guide/recaps-and-tags). That
context is exactly what the coach reads when you ask for a review — follow the
[Roadmap](/docs/roadmap) to see the rest come together.
