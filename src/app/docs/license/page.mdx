It's provided as-is, without warranty. You run it yourself, so you're responsible for your
own data and backups.

## Why open source

A trading journal holds some of your most sensitive records — account history, positions,
timestamps. Being open source means you (or anyone you trust) can read exactly what the app
does with that data. There's no hidden telemetry to take on faith, and the project can't
disappear behind a paywall or a shut-down server.

## Use & responsibility

This is a journaling and review tool — **not** financial advice, trade recommendations, risk
or tax advice, or brokerage software. It's provided as-is; verify your own data, protect your
own broker exports, and use it at your own risk.
